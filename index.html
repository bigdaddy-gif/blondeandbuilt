🥗 Nutrition Guide

Supplements are 30% of the equation. Food timing is the other 70%. This is what to eat and exactly when.

🍳 Pre-Workout Meal (1–2 hrs before)

  • Fast-digesting carbs + lean protein — oatmeal + eggs, rice + chicken, banana + Greek yogurt
  • Avoid high fat and high fiber — they slow digestion and kill energy mid-session
  • Drink 16–20 oz water before you even step in the gym
  • Training fasted? Take BCAAs at minimum to protect muscle tissue

🏆 Post-Workout Meal (within 30–60 min)

  • 25–40g protein immediately — whey, eggs, chicken, or lean meat
  • Fast carbs to replenish glycogen — white rice, fruit, or a sports drink
  • Skip high fats right after — they slow down nutrient absorption into muscle
  • Rehydrate aggressively — replace every ounce of sweat lost during training

😴 Rest Day Nutrition

  • Reduce carbs slightly — less output means less glycogen needed
  • Keep protein HIGH (0.7–1g per lb) — muscle repair continues on rest days
  • Anti-inflammatory foods — berries, leafy greens, fatty fish, turmeric
  • Zinc, magnesium, and vitamin C accelerate overnight recovery
