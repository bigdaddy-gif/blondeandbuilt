🥗 Nutrition Guide

What to eat and when — the exact fuel plan to build muscle fast

🍳 PRE-WORKOUT MEAL (1–2 hrs before)

Fuel the Work, Not the Crash

  • Eat fast-digesting carbs + lean protein — oatmeal + eggs, rice + chicken, banana + Greek yogurt
  • Avoid high-fat and high-fiber foods — they slow digestion and kill energy mid-session
  • Hydrate with 16–20 oz water before you even step in the gym
  • If training fasted: at minimum take BCAAs to protect muscle tissue
🏆 POST-WORKOUT MEAL (within 30–60 min)

Your Muscles Are Open for Business

  • Priority: 25–40g protein immediately — whey, eggs, or lean meat
  • Add fast carbs to replenish glycogen: white rice, fruit, or a sports drink
  • Skip high fats right after — they slow nutrient absorption into muscle
  • Chocolate milk is underrated and science-backed for recovery
  • Rehydrate aggressively — replace every ounce of sweat lost
😴 REST DAY EATING

Rest Days = Repair Days, Not Cheat Days

  • Slightly reduce carbs — less output means less need for glycogen replenishment
  • Keep protein HIGH (0.7–1g per lb bodyweight) to sustain muscle repair
  • Load up on anti-inflammatory foods: berries, leafy greens, fatty fish, turmeric
  • Focus on micronutrients — zinc, magnesium, and vitamin C accelerate recovery
  • Consistency beats restriction every time — don't binge, don't starve
